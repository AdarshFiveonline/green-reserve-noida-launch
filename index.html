Green Reserve

Green Reserve Wish Town along the Noida-Greater Noida is a diverse 1162 acres that combines sophisticated city living with a friendly neighborhood atmosphere. The residential community has been seamlessly interwoven into a 18+9 Hole Graham Cooke Golf facility presenting a scenic and natural ecosystem for living as a biggest USP. Enjoying good connectivity and location advantages with respect to the city master plan, the project is approximately 5 minutes from the start of the Noida to Greater Noida Expressway (Mahamaya Flyover), around 15 minutes from South Delhi and approximately 25 minutes from Central Delhi. It is one of the largest integrated township in India with a 70% green cover.
